Saridon

What is Saridon?

Saridon is an analgesic commonly used for headaches, common cold, and menstrual cramps. It is an over-the-counter medication that provides instant relief to patients complaining of aches associated with fever, toothache, and arthritis-related aches. Saridon is composed of three molecules, Paracetamol, Propyphenazone, and Caffeine. These molecules have slightly different functions and, together, show excellent efficacy in alleviating pain. Saridon is commonly available in a dosage of 50 mg in a 10-tablet blister pack

What are the uses of Saridon?

Saridon is a class of NSAID molecules that help reduce body temperature and inflammation by blocking the chemical messengers in the body that cause this inflammation.
